## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your environment">
    Choose the path that fits your needs: [Cloud Sandbox](/quickstart/cloud-sandbox) for instant access, [Docker](/quickstart/local-docker) for local development, or [AWS/Kubernetes](/deployment/overview) for production deployment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a payment processor">
    Activate a connector in the Control Center or via API. Hyperswitch supports 100+ processors including Stripe, Adyen, Braintree, PayPal, and more. See the [Connectors guide](/connectors/activate-connector).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first payment">
    Use the SDK, Postman collection, or API directly to run a test transaction. Follow the [First Payment guide](/quickstart/first-payment) to see the complete flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Hyperswitch is fully open-source under the Apache 2.0 license. You can self-host on AWS, GCP, Azure, or any Kubernetes cluster, or use the managed cloud offering at [app.hyperswitch.io](https://app.hyperswitch.io).
</Note>
