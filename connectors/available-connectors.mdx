Hyperswitch supports 50+ connectors across payment processors, payout providers, fraud tools, and authentication providers. The sections below describe each connector's supported payment methods and any connector-specific setup requirements.

<Note>
  The most up-to-date connector and payment method matrix is available at [integrations.hyperswitch.io](https://integrations.hyperswitch.io). The list below covers the major processors with additional detail.
</Note>

## Payment processors

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Stripe">
    Stripe is a full-stack payments platform for internet businesses of all sizes. It supports cards, wallets, and a broad range of local payment methods.

    **Supported payment methods:** Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, UnionPay), Apple Pay, Google Pay, Link, SEPA direct debit, ACH, BACS, iDEAL, Sofort, Bancontact, EPS, Giropay, Klarna, Afterpay, Affirm, and more.

    **Required credentials:** Secret Key (starts with `sk_live_` for production, `sk_test_` for sandbox)

    **Setup notes:**
    - Request that Stripe enable raw card data handling for your account (required for Hyperswitch to tokenize cards). Contact Stripe support to enable this.
    - Configure webhooks in your Stripe dashboard under **Developers → Webhooks**. Create a new endpoint pointing to the Hyperswitch webhook URL found in **Connectors → Stripe** in the Control Center.
    - Ensure payment methods are enabled in both Hyperswitch and your Stripe dashboard under **Settings → Payments → Payment methods**.

    **Test card:** `4242 4242 4242 4242`, expiry `12/25`, CVV `100`
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Adyen">
    Adyen is a global payments company with strong coverage for both online and in-person payments across Europe, North America, and Asia-Pacific.

    **Supported payment methods:** Cards (all major networks), Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, iDEAL, Sofort, Klarna, Bancontact, EPS, Giropay, and many local payment methods.

    **Required credentials:** API Key and Merchant Account ID (found in your Adyen dashboard under **Developers → API credentials**)

    **Setup notes:**
    - Contact Adyen support (`support@adyen.com`) to enable raw card data handling for your account. This is required for Hyperswitch to process card payments through Adyen.
    - Configure a standard webhook in **Developers → Webhooks** in your Adyen dashboard. Copy the HMAC key and enter it as the Source Verification Key in the Hyperswitch Control Center.
    - For Klarna, the following fields are required or strongly recommended for good conversion: `email`, `billing.first_name`, `billing.last_name`, `billing.city`, `billing.country`, `billing.line1`, `billing.zip`, and `order_details`. A customer record (`customer_id`) must also be created via the Hyperswitch API.

    **Country and currency coverage for key methods:**

    | Method | Regions | Currencies |
    |---|---|---|
    | Apple Pay | AU, NZ, JP, HK, SG, BR, US, CA, GB, and most of Europe | AUD, CHF, CAD, EUR, GBP, HKD, SGD, USD |
    | Google Pay | AU, NZ, JP, HK, SG, BR, US, CA, GB, and most of Europe | All enabled on your Adyen account |
    | PayPal | AU, NZ, JP, HK, US, CA, GB, BR, and most of Europe and Asia | AUD, BRL, CAD, EUR, GBP, USD, and 15+ others |
    | iDEAL | NL | EUR |
    | Klarna | AU, AT, BE, CA, CZ, DK, FI, FR, DE, IT, NL, NO, PL, ES, SE, CH, GB, US | AUD, EUR, CAD, GBP, USD, and others |

    **Test card:** `4000 0200 0000 0000`, expiry `03/30`, CVV `737`

    <Note>
      In the Adyen sandbox environment, automatic capture does not work for Klarna and PayPal — payments must be explicitly captured before refunds can be processed. This is an Adyen sandbox behavior; contact Adyen support if the same issue occurs in production.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="PayPal">
    PayPal provides a widely recognized digital wallet and payment gateway. It supports redirect-based and native SDK payment flows.

    **Supported payment methods:** PayPal Wallet, Venmo (US), cards (via PayPal's processing).

    **Required credentials:** Client ID and Client Secret (found in your PayPal developer dashboard under **My Apps & Credentials**)

    **Setup notes:**
    - Ensure the payment methods you enable in Hyperswitch match those configured in your PayPal dashboard.
    - PayPal uses OAuth-based authentication. Hyperswitch exchanges your client credentials for an access token on each request.

## 3DS authentication connectors

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Netcetera">
    Netcetera is a 3D Secure server and access control server (ACS) provider, enabling external or standalone 3DS authentication flows.

    **Use cases:**
    - Standalone 3DS authentication before payment authorization
    - Native 3DS for mobile applications
    - Decoupled 3DS flows

    **Required credentials:** API Key and Endpoint URL (obtained from Netcetera)

    Netcetera is configured as an authentication connector, not a payment processor. It works alongside your existing payment processors to provide 3DS frictionless or challenge flows.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
